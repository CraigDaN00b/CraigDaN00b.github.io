Cakes

Flour-based baked sweets

Cakes are sweet and often indulgent baked desserts that typically consist of a mixture of flour, sugar, eggs, and butter or oil. They come in various shapes, sizes, and flavors, ranging from classic vanilla and chocolate to more elaborate options like red velvet or fruit-infused varieties. Cakes are commonly enjoyed on special occasions such as birthdays, weddings, or celebrations, often adorned with frosting, icing, or other decorative elements.

  • Variations: Chocolate, Vanilla, Ube, Red Velvet, Black Forest
  • Price: Around ₱550 - ₱1250

Coffee

Brewed beverage made from coffee beans

Coffee is a popular beverage made from roasted coffee beans, which are the seeds of the Coffea plant. The drink is known for its stimulating effects due to the presence of caffeine, a natural stimulant. People around the world enjoy coffee for its rich flavors, diverse brewing methods, and the energizing boost it provides.

  • Variations: Classic, Strong, Brown, Latté, Cappucino
  • Price: ₱50 for Mini, ₱100 for Regular, ₱150 for Large

Milk Tea

Beverage mainly composed of tea and milk

Milk tea is a popular beverage that combines tea with milk and sugar to create a flavorful and creamy drink. Originating from various Asian cultures, it is often prepared by brewing strong tea, usually black or green tea, and then blending it with sweetened condensed milk, evaporated milk, or regular milk. The result is a versatile and comforting beverage enjoyed worldwide, with variations in preparation methods and flavors across different regions.

  • Variations: Classic, Green Tea, Chocolate, Taro, Mango, Winter Melon
  • Price: ₱75 for Mini, ₱125 for Regular, ₱175 for Large

Breads

Food made of flour and water

Bread is a staple food that has been a dietary mainstay across cultures for centuries. It is typically made from flour, water, yeast, and salt, with variations in ingredients and preparation methods leading to a diverse range of bread types worldwide. Through processes like fermentation, dough is leavened, creating the characteristic texture and taste that make bread a versatile and beloved element in cuisines globally.

  • Variations: Loaf Bread, Buttered Toast, Cream-Filled Bread
  • Price: ₱200 on Loaf Bread, ₱150 for a 10pc pack of Buttered Toast, ₱10 a piece of Cream-Filled Bread

Ice Cream

Frozen desserts with assorted flavors

Ice cream is a frozen dessert made from a mixture of cream, sugar, and flavorings such as fruit, chocolate, or vanilla. The ingredients are churned together while freezing, creating a smooth and creamy texture. It is a popular treat enjoyed worldwide in various flavors and forms, from cones to sundaes.

  • Variations: Chocolate, Vanilla, Mango, Strawberry, Mint Choco Chip, Cookies and Cream
  • Price: ₱100 for Regular, ₱150 for Large

Biscuits

Sweet baked products

Biscuits are baked, bread-like products that are typically made from a combination of flour, fat (such as butter or shortening), leavening agents (such as baking powder), and liquid (usually milk or buttermilk). The dough is rolled or dropped onto a baking sheet and then baked until golden brown, resulting in a soft and flaky texture. Biscuits are a versatile food item, enjoyed as a side dish with savory meals or sweetened for a delightful breakfast or dessert treat.

  • Variations: Chocolate, Vanilla, Ube, Red Velvet, Black Forest
  • Price: Around ₱550 - ₱1250
